No usage supervision

When you use the AI Hub, Forward College does not collect, store or have access to any data from messages. Your activity is private and not used for monitoring or evaluation.

An optional resource

Usage of the AI Hub is optional. The AI Hub is available for all staff members as a free alternative to other AI services, but staff is encouraged to explore and use any AI tools that suit their professional tasks.

Secure and confidential

The AI Hub is safer to use than most free tools. Data inserted in the AI Hub is secure, not used by AI companies to train their models, and protected by low-data-retention or zero-data-retention agreements.

Information Governance & Data Privacy

1. What is the Staff AI Hub?

It's a platform that Forward uses to provide enterprise-grade AI access to all staff. In comparison to the regular AI Apps (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) the AI Hub offers better security for handling sensitive data, unlimited and unrestricted access to AI models, and customized bots to meet specific staff needs.

2. How to activate my account?

Simply register using your Forward Email account. Upon your registration, one of the AI Hub admins will receive a notification and will proceed with authorizing your account - which shouldn't last more than a day.
Until we can verify you are a member of staff and activate your account, you will have access to 50 message credits to start testing the AI Hub. Once your account is activated, you will have unlimited access to the AI Hub.

3. What can I use it for?

The AI Hub is available for staff to use for all professional and personal AI-related needs. Forward does not have any access to staff message content and does not monitor usage by staff.

4. Do I need to use the AI Hub?

No, usage of the AI Hub is optional. The AI Hub is available for all staff members, but staff is encouraged to explore and use other AI tools that best support their personal efficiency and tasks.

5. Is my data confidential?

Our AI provider (pickaxe.co) collects and stores messages and usage information to provide its services. Their service is GDPR and SOC2 compliant, and provides a Trust Center for transparency.
The AI team itself does not collect, store or have access to any data from messages. Members of the AI team have also signed non-disclosure agreements and are strictly forbidden from pursuing access to staff data.

6. Is my data used to train models?

Your data is handled by our AI provider (pickaxe.co) according to their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. In it, you will find that external AI companies (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) get access to your requests and documents to provide AI answers, but with commitments to no model training and data deletion.

7. Who has oversight?

The AI Team Administrators (Devlin and Leo) are responsible for managing and maintaining Pickaxe. They do not have access to your messages and are supervising the health of the website through cost and usage metrics provided by pickaxe.co.

8. What control do I have?

You maintain full rights to access your metadata or request account deletion. For any governance-related queries, please contact the team through our joint email account ai@forward-college.eu.
